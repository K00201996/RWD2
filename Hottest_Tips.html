Fallout76

Carry Weight Tips & Tricks

With the stash limit so low and the amount of hoarders in this game so high, here is some advice I've found helps lighten the load when you're on those country roads.

*Every point of strength adds 5 points to your carry weight.

*The effects of food, alcohol and chems stack. You may have 1 of each active at a time.

*Most alcohol adds 1 strength, whiskey adds 2, with 2 points in the Party Boy/Girl perk the effects are tripled.

*Buffout raises your strength by 2 for awhile, other variants (like psychobuff) increase it by 3.

*Grilled Radstag increases carry weight by 20, get hunting!

*All Power armor raises your strength by 11, Excavator Power Armor (which can be found toward the bottom of the ash heap), adds an extra 100 points to carry weight.

*Power armor frames weigh 10 points, regardless of what is equiped to them. Placing all of your armor on a frame and picking up the frame will weigh you down a lot less than carrying each piece individually.

*Bulking materials at a tinker's workbench reduces their weight.

*Ammo and chems have weight, with no NPCs to cheat out of their caps, do you need 120 mentats?

*Without perks, missiles weigh 3 points and mini nukes weigh 6, way too heavy to be carry around.

*Scrap any weapons and armor you don't use, it will unlock useful mods, equip the scrapper perk for more materials!

*Armor pieces can be modded to improve carry weight or take up less space.

*Perks that decrease an items weight only work when that item is in your inventory, it will still take up the full amount of space in your stash.

*Always take the Pack Rat & Strong Back perks, you'll thank me.

If you think of any more, let Us know below!
